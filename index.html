Who We Are

AfroXDance is a revival of The University of Waterloo's Afro Fusion Dance Club. We are an African inspired dance club that plans on teaching original choreography to all dancers starting in Fall 2021.

Future Vision

The future of ADC is one where all University of Waterloo's studetns feel encouraged to express themselves. Collaboration from other dance clubs are definite and aim to diversify dancers skill set and share culture amongst each other.
